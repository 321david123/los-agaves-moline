Who We Are

A family table on
16th Street.

Los Agaves Mexican Grill is a family-owned, full-service restaurant and cantina — part of a small Quad Cities group. Our 16th Street kitchen pours out a big, classic Mexican menu: sizzling fajitas, golden chimichangas, fresh seafood, sizzling T-bone steaks and generous combination plates.

Every table starts with complimentary chips and fresh salsa, every plate comes generous, and the full bar keeps the margaritas flowing. The room is casual, lively and family-friendly — the kind of neighborhood spot where the staff knows the regulars and the kitchen never skimps.
